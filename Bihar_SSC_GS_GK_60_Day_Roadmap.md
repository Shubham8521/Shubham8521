# Bihar SSC GS/GK — 60-Day High-Yield Roadmap (Working Professional Edition)

**Designed for:** Candidate working 9 AM–6 PM, 3–4 hours weekdays, longer weekends, 2 months to exam.
**Target:** Maximize marks in **General Studies + General Knowledge only** (no Math/Reasoning/English here).
**Built on:** BSSC Inter-Level + BSSC CGL + Bihar Police PYQ trend analysis.

---

## Exam Trend Snapshot (Why this roadmap is built this way)

| Section | Approx. Weight in BSSC GS/GK | Highest-yield zones |
|---|---|---|
| Bihar GK | 25–30% | Ancient Bihar (Magadh/Maurya/Gupta), Bihar geography & rivers, CM/Governors, Bihar schemes, festivals/dances, freedom movement in Bihar |
| Indian History | 15–20% | Ancient (Buddhism, Jainism, Mauryas, Guptas), Modern (1857, Champaran, Quit India, INC sessions) |
| Geography | 10–12% | Indian physical features, rivers, climate, agriculture, minerals + Bihar geography |
| Polity | 10–12% | Preamble, FR/DPSP, Parliament, President, Bihar Legislative Council, Panchayati Raj |
| Science | 12–15% | NCERT 6–10: Human body, diseases, motion/force, light, electricity, periodic table, acids/bases |
| Static GK | 8–10% | Dances, sports, awards, books-authors, defence, organisations |
| Current Affairs | 10–12% | Last 6 months — Bihar-state news, appointments, sports, schemes, summits |

---

## Daily Time Architecture

**Weekday (Mon–Fri, ~3.5 hrs)**
- **Morning (6:30–7:45 AM, 1 hr 15 min):** New topic study (fresh brain = conceptual subjects)
- **Evening (8:00–10:30 PM, 2 hrs 30 min):** Topic continuation + MCQs + 15–20 min current affairs + revision of previous day

**Saturday (~6 hrs):** Deep study + sectional test + PYQs
**Sunday (~6–7 hrs):** Full GS/GK mock + error analysis + weak-topic revision

## High-Yield "Must-Know" Compressed Lists (Print these)

### Bihar GK Hot-100 (touch every week)
- All Bihar CMs in order (Krishna Singh → Nitish Kumar tenures)
- All Bihar Governors (current + last 5)
- 38 districts + 9 divisions
- Major rivers + their tributaries (Kosi = "Sorrow of Bihar")
- Folk dances: Jat-Jatin, Jhijhia, Bidesia, Paika, Kajari, Jhumari
- Festivals: Chhath, Sonepur Mela, Pitrapaksha Mela, Rajgir Mahotsav
- Saat Nishchay 1 & 2 components
- Bihar in 1857: Kunwar Singh (Jagdishpur)
- Champaran Satyagraha 1917, Buxar 1764, Vaishali (1st Republic)
- Magadha capitals: Rajgir → Pataliputra
- Nalanda, Vikramshila, Odantapuri universities
- Bihar Vidhan Parishad (oldest, 1913 origin)

### Polity Must-Know
- Preamble keywords + 42nd Amendment changes
- Articles 12–35 (FR), 36–51 (DPSP), 51A (FD)
- Schedule list (1st to 12th)
- Important Articles: 14, 19, 21, 32, 226, 356, 370, 368

### Science Hot-50
- Vitamin deficiency table (A–K)
- Diseases: viral / bacterial / protozoal classification
- Newton's laws + units (SI)
- Periodic table: groups, alkali metals, noble gases
- Acids/bases pH, common chemical names (NaCl, NaHCO3, CaCO3, etc.)
- Human body: bones (206), heart chambers, blood groups

### Static GK Quick Hits
- Classical dances (state ↔ dance)
- Cricket/Football/Hockey trophies
- Bharat Ratna last 10 awardees
- HQ of UN bodies, BRICS, SAARC, ASEAN
- Defence exercises (India + partner country)
